EirGate Account and Data Deletion

EirGate users may delete their account from within the EirGate app under Settings → Delete Account.

Alternatively, users may request deletion by emailing support@eirgate.com from the email address registered to their account.

When an account is deleted, the user profile, gate assignments and associated personal information will be removed. Certain security, transaction or access records may be retained where required for legal, security or operational purposes.

Deletion requests will normally be processed within 30 days.
